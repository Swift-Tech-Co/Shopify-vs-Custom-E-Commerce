Frequently Asked Questions

Is Shopify or a custom e-commerce platform better?

Shopify is better for most retailers with under 10,000 SKUs who need fast time-to-market. Custom e-commerce is better for complex pricing logic, B2B buyer portals, subscription billing, or stores processing over $10M annually where platform fees become significant.

How does Shopify TCO compare to custom?

Shopify TCO at $1M revenue: $13,000 to $41,000/year (platform plus transaction fees plus apps). Custom e-commerce: no transaction fees, but $40,000 to $150,000 upfront plus $8,000 to $24,000/year maintenance. Break-even typically at $2M to $5M annual revenue.

What can custom e-commerce do that Shopify cannot?

Custom advantages: complex B2B pricing (customer-specific catalogues, tiered pricing, quote workflows), unlimited checkout customization, custom subscription logic, unique product configurators, full headless architecture control, and zero transaction fees.
